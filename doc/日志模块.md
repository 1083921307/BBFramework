# 虣虣游戏框架教程 日志输出模块(让游戏开发变得更简单)

- 普通日志输出

```typescript
bb.log(msg: string): void;

bb.log2(msg: string): void;
```
---

- 重要日志输出

```typescript
bb.info(msg: string): void;

bb.info2(msg: string): void;

bb.info3(msg: string): void;
```
---

- 警告日志输出

```typescript
bb.warn(msg: string): void;

```
---

- 错误日志输出


```typescript
bb.error(msg: string): void;

```
